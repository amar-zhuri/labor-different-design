SERVICES
We offer a comprehensive range of medical laboratory services including:

1. Complete Blood Count (CBC)
   - Comprehensive blood analysis including cell counts, hemoglobin levels, and other important parameters

2. Cardiac Profile
   - Heart health assessment, cholesterol, and triglycerides testing

3. Hormonal Tests
   - Hormone level analysis for endocrine disorder diagnosis

4. Clinical Biochemistry
   - Analysis of enzymes, proteins, and chemical substances in blood for organ function assessment

5. Infectious Disease Testing
   - Accurate diagnosis for HIV, Hepatitis A/B/C, and other infectious diseases

6. Tumor Markers
   - Specialized tests for early cancer detection and treatment monitoring

7. Allergy Panels
   - Identification of various allergies for healthier living

8. Diabetes Panel
   - HbA1c, glucose tolerance, insulin levels for diabetes management

9. Complete Thyroid Panel
   - TSH, T3, T4, Anti-TPO antibodies for comprehensive thyroid assessment

10. Vitamins & Minerals
    - B12, D3, Iron, Folate and other essential minerals for health

11. Autoimmune Diseases
    - ANA, RF, Anti-CCP and other autoimmune markers for accurate diagnosis

12. Heavy Metals
    - Testing for lead, mercury, cadmium and other toxic metals

13. Urinalysis
    - Complete urine testing for kidney function assessment, infections, and metabolic disorders

14. Electronic Results 24/7
    - Patients can access their results online anytime from phone, tablet, or computer
